Comprehensive Healthcare Services

World-class medical care tailored for Zimbabwe's diverse healthcare needs, from preventive care to specialized treatments.

General Consultation

Comprehensive primary healthcare by qualified Zimbabwean doctors, including chronic disease management for diabetes, hypertension, and other conditions common in our region.

Diagnostics & Imaging

Comprehensive diagnostic services including X-rays, CT scans, ultrasound, ECG, malaria testing, HIV screening, TB diagnostics, and full laboratory panels.

Pediatric Care

Specialized child healthcare including immunizations per Zimbabwe's EPI schedule, growth monitoring, and treatment of common childhood illnesses.

Maternal Health

Comprehensive antenatal care, safe delivery services, and postnatal support following WHO and Zimbabwe Ministry of Health guidelines.

Full-Service Pharmacy

Licensed pharmacy with essential medicines list compliance, prescription dispensing, and pharmaceutical consultation by registered pharmacists.

Emergency Services

24/7 emergency care for acute conditions, trauma stabilization, and urgent medical interventions with ambulance referral services when needed.
